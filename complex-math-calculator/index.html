Complex Numbers

Master the fundamentals of complex number arithmetic including addition, multiplication, conjugates, and modulus calculations.

  • Addition: (a₁+ib₁) + (a₂+ib₂)
  • Multiplication: (a₁+ib₁)(a₂+ib₂)
  • Conjugate: z = a+ib → z̄ = a−ib
  • Modulus: |z| = √(a²+b²)
  • Key Identity: z·z̄ = |z|²
